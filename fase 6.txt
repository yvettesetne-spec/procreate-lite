### 0. Smooth Stroke Rendering (Critical - Do First)
**Problem:** Current brush draws straight line segments between pointer events, causing zigzag on freehand strokes with generic stylus.

**Solution:** Replace `lineTo` with quadratic BĂ©zier curves between midpoints:
- For each 3 consecutive points, calculate the midpoint between point[i] and point[i+1]
- Use `quadraticCurveTo(point[i].x, point[i].y, midX, midY)` 
- This creates smooth curves that follow the user's hand naturally
- Apply to all brushes: solid, pencil, airbrush, marker
- Keep stabilization slider as additional smoothing on top

**Files:** `main.js` â€” `draw()` function, brush rendering branches
